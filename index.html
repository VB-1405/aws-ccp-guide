// The Story

Why These Guides Exist

Built while studying, published after passing.

After passing the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner exam in May 2026, I turned my entire prep into a free, structured study guide that anyone can use.

I'm a Cybersecurity MS student at CSUDH and I believe the best way to truly understand something is to teach it. Every guide here is written from the perspective of someone who just passed the real exam — so the content reflects what actually matters.

The Security+ guide embeds Professor Messer's free videos directly into each topic — so you have context before reading the notes. No hunting YouTube. No switching tabs.

This platform grows with every cert I earn. Watch this space.
